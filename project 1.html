Expanding the horizons

To see the sky (or NEBO as we call it in Ukraine) in this city, lift your head!
NEBO has no boundaries, you just have to look beyond the horizon. We are against monotony and standard patterns. NEBO is always different: rain and aggressive clouds give way to a bright sunset and monochrome calmness.

Our values are honesty of opinions, the courage to be different, greed for new things, excitement in the creation, and willpower.

Idiocentric methodology

We put standard patterns and marketing channels aside. We create ideas that go beyond what is expected. Everyone craves for a miracle, but only by going to the ends of the Earth, one can see Aurora.
